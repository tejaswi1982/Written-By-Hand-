Day 7 · This morning

Choose how you want to write today.

There is no wrong choice. The page is patient.

01 · Freewrite

Let the hand lead.

Write whatever comes. Don't edit. Don't judge. Just keep the hand moving.
02 · Copy

Borrow good rhythm.

Copy a page from a book, newspaper, magazine, poem, or anything you like. Focus on form and rhythm.
03 · Prompt

Begin with a question.

Let the app give you a simple prompt for today.

Whatever you write, only the form of your handwriting is reviewed. Never the meaning.
